COVID-19 vaccine rollout in Alberta

First Immunization

The first 3,900 doses of the Pfizer vaccine will arrive in Alberta next week, and immunizations for ICU doctors and nurses, respiratory therapists and long-term care workers are expected to begin Dec. 16.

Number of People get immunized first

Around 1,950 people will be immunized on week DEC 16

Immunization Location

Shots will be given at the two initial shipment locations in Edmonton and Calgary.
